Help File

Q:    Puri doesn't work for me correctly?
A:    I can imagine just two cases when that can happen.
  1. You don't run Puri on Haiku alpha 4 or better.
  2. Your cpu doesn't support SSE2. (E.g. your cpu is older than pentium 4)
    • Solution: Contact me, and I will give you a "generic" version, that doesn't make use of SSE2
Q:   Are there keyboard shortcuts other than the ones  found in the menubar?
A:    Yes. For more detailed informations please visit   Puri - Master of Chess 

Q:   I want to play online chess, what I can do?
A:   Register an account here. At startup enter your username and password and choose "Play Online"

Q:   I want to support the development of Puri, what options I have?
A:   You have a few opportunities to support the development
  • With c++ code.
    • Especially the 3D Mode needs more features. You need to know c++ and OpenGL for that.
    • The online chess mode needs a lot more features. The commands of FICS need to accesible to the users via a GUI , a la ChessBase.
    • The GUI needs more features related to chess analysis. Multiple engine support.
    • Database capabilities are missing yet.
  • With artistic work
    • New 3D chess models in 3ds format.
    • More Vector Icons (hvif)
  • With money
    • Please consider making a donation. Any amout is welcome, even 1 euro or less. It's not just a financiar support, but also a moral support. It helps to know and see that there are people interested in your work and are also ready to contribute.
